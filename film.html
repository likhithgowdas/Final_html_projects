Mark

Mark is a 2023 Indian Kannada-language action thriller film directed by debutant A. P. Arjun and produced by Vijay Kiragandur under the Hombale Films banner. The film stars Kiccha Sudeepa in the titular role, along with Vijay Kartikeya, Megha Akash and Shivani Surve in prominent roles. The music is composed by Ajaneesh Loknath, while Vaidy S. handled the cinematography and Deepu S. Kumar edited the film. Mark was released on 7 December 2023, where it received mixed to positive reviews from critics and became a commercial success at the box office.
